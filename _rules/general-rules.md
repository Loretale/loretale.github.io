*All rules are subject to change and may be applied retroactively. They may be applied to any behavior on Loretale services, or related community services.*

1. Stay civil.
2. Do not harass other users. This may warrant a ban particularly when directed at out of game factors such as racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, etc., as well as performing acts like doxxing other users.
3. You must be at least 16 years old.
4. If you see someone breaking rules, you are obligated to report this with a ticket in ⁠tickets. Failure to do this may result in a ban.
5. NSFW content is not allowed on platform.
6. Distributing malware or otherwise illegal content is not allowed.
7. Selling in game goods for out of game services / resources is not allowed.
8. The use of hacked or automated clients is not allowed.
9. Advertisement of unrelated platforms / servers / communities is prohibited.
10. The use of VPN / VPS to connect to services is generally prohibited. If you need to use one, open a request in a ⁠ticket in the discord.
11. You may only use one account.
