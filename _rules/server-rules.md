1. PvP, or otherwise mechanically killing players, is not permitted for the time being.
2. Griefing, or otherwise stealing or breaking things another player have built, is not permitted for the time being.
3. The server follows a medieval / fantasy aesthetic. Builds, characters, etc. should follow this aesthetic.
4. Landscars (builds that are without purpose and/or do not fit the aesthetics of the server) may be removed by staff.
5. Abusing glitches and exploits is not permitted. Please report them by creating a ticket.
6. Accounts must not be shared with other players.
